White cat

Cat is built for agility and speed. To being able to perform his/her abilities to its best, strength or better muscle power is required to do so. The cat's anatomy and sharp claws give him/her the ability to climb trees or any other surface with ease.

  1. White cats are usually found in North America.
  2. These White cats have ability to climb trees using sharp claws and paws.
  3. There is a belief that White cats live for 9 lifes.
  4. They are very good at senses like smelling and hearing
  5. White cats are famous for their fast foot and long visibility
