How to make pizza step by step:-

  1. Start with a medium bowl that's been lightly coated with olive oil.
  2. Add warm water (about 110 degrees F), dry yeast and sugar.
  3. Make a Well in the Center of the Dough.
  4. Knead the Dough
    1. Cover the Dough in Plastic Wrap.
    2. Check to See If the Dough Is Done.
  5. Divide the Dough.
  6. Top the Pizza and Bake
